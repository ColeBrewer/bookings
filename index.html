About Us

We are a Booking Facility Brand for Hamilton Boys High School. HBHS offers a wide range of facilites including halls, gyms, fields, turfs, pools, The Dome and The Fitness Center. Now through this new website HBHS Facility Booking staff members and the public are able to book any facility at any time through this booking website. You submit your details of your booking to the facility manager Mr Mccarthy who will approve or deny your booking request based off eligibility and other calendar events. When your booking is approved he will add it to the the Calendar and your time will be booked.
